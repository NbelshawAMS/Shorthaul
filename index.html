How Impact Is Calculated
Before: office and company revenue are scaled to a $6M monthly Texas baseline, then profit is calculated at a 15% margin.

After: baseline revenue stays in place and recovered truck-days are added at $1,400 per day. Profit on those recovered truck-days is modeled at a 25% margin.

Important: this model is intended to show operational efficiency from reducing deadhead and improving backhaul matching. A 70% local baseline is reserved first at each office, and long-haul activity is constrained to the remaining capacity. It is not assuming brand-new statewide demand is created.
